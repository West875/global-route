📍 4. Location Data

Global Route requests access to your device's GPS location. This is used for:

  • Calculating the nearest job site and optimizing your route
  • Geocoding photos (attaching GPS coordinates to site photos)
  • Displaying your position on the map

Location access is optional. You can deny or revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. The app will still function but routing features will be limited.

We do not continuously track your location in the background. Location is only accessed when the app is actively open and in use.

📸 5. Photos & Camera Access

The app requests camera access for:

  • Profile selfie — Used as your team identifier and avatar
  • Site photos — Before/after documentation at job sites

Photos are stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage and are only visible to members of your workspace. You can delete your own photos at any time.

Camera access is optional. You can use the app without taking photos.

🎙️ 6. Voice Notes & Microphone Access

The app requests microphone access to record voice notes at job sites. Voice recordings are:

  • Stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Only accessible to members of your workspace
  • Deletable by the author at any time

Microphone access is optional and only activated when you tap the record button.

🔐 7. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Encryption in transit — All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at rest — Data stored in Firebase is encrypted at rest using Google Cloud's default encryption
  • Authentication — All data access requires authenticated user sessions via Firebase Authentication
  • Workspace isolation — Data is scoped to individual workspaces; team members in one workspace cannot access another workspace's data
  • Payment security — All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified processor. We never store, process, or transmit credit card numbers

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

⏳ 8. Data Retention

  • Account data — Retained as long as your account is active
  • Site completions, notes, photos — Retained as long as the workspace is active
  • Voice notes — Retained until deleted by the author or workspace is deleted
  • Payment records — Retained as required by law (typically 7 years for financial records)

When you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
